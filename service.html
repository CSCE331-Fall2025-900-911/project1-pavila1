Community Services & Global Awareness

Beyond core engineering, my work is driven by a deep commitment to **community and inclusivity**, stemming from my identity as a **first-generation Latina and a female engineer**. I am acutely aware of the digital and informational gaps that exist in many communities. Therefore, I dedicate my expertise to bridging these divides. This involves volunteering technical knowledge and time to local organizations, including **food and clothing banks**, and supporting initiatives for children during holidays. Looking ahead, I am focused on providing **advanced technological assistance**—such as developing data management systems, creating introductory coding workshops, or offering technical consultation—to local schools and non-profits. My goal is to apply my engineering foundation to not only solve complex technical problems but also to build stronger, more equitable, and more informed communities around the world.

Areas of focus for technological support:

  • Life Steps
  • Community Service Program (TAMU)
  • Fodd Pantries
  • Aggieland Humane Society
