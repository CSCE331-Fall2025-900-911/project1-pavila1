Services & Beyond: My Future Vision

My goal is to use my engineering skills to create technologies that promote sustainability and improve quality of life. I aspire to innovate in areas such as medical technology, informational systems, and public safety, ensuring that progress serves people and communities. Ultimately, I want to be part of the movement that leverages technology to address humanity’s greatest challenges and build a better future.

My Personal Drive for Service

Because I know firsthand how life-changing these opportunities can be, I feel a deep responsibility to make sure others have access to the same support. Volunteering with organizations that provide food, clothing, and mentorship is more than just service to me—it’s a chance to offer stability, dignity, and hope to people who truly need it. What excites me most is mentoring young students, especially those from underrepresented backgrounds, and showing them that despite the barriers they face, they belong in STEM and can achieve greatness.

I carry a deep sense of responsibility to ensure others can access the same opportunities that transformed my own path. For me, volunteering is not abstract service; it's a direct way to provide the stability, dignity, and hope I know is essential. When I work with organizations providing food, clothing, or mentorship, I see a chance to build up people and communities that genuinely need support. What truly fuels me is mentoring young students, especially those from underrepresented backgrounds, and showing them that their unique perspective is a superpower. I want them to know, with absolute certainty, that despite the barriers, they belong in STEM and are capable of achieving greatness.

My goal is to one day return to communities like my own—through STEM initiatives, mentorship, and social programs—and provide the same encouragement and resources that once changed my path. To me, giving back is not just service; it is an investment in the future, an opportunity to uplift the next generation, and a way to create stronger, more inclusive communities. These programs matter deeply—they reach further than we often realize, offering hope, stability, and possibility where it is needed most. I know this because they transformed my life, and I am determined to continue that cycle by helping others discover their own potential and pursue futures they may have never imagined.
